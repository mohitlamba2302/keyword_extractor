Big data analysis of youth tobacco smoking trends in the United States
As large amounts of data are now generated in the healthcare industry, big data technologies are used to process these data. Tobacco smoking has been significant among the youth in the United States. In this research, we use big data techniques such as R and Tableau to explore the tobacco smoking trends among the youth in the United States. Results indicate that there is more number of youth male smokers than youth female smokers. The results also indicate that more than 51 percent of current youth smokers want to quit smoking.
